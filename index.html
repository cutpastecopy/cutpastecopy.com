About Cut Paste Copy

Cut Paste Copy is a student run graphic design business which is run through New Milford High School. We are taught to use the Adobe Creative Suite programs, mostly focusing on Photoshop and Illustrator. To practice our graphic design skills, we reach out to the community for real world experience.

For our past community projects we have created: business cards, T- shirt designs, and among other things. If you would like to see some of the many projects we have created, please vist our projects page. If you would like us to create something for you, feel free to go to our contact page to email us about any projects you would like us to create.

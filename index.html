About Cut, Paste & Copy

Cut, Paste & Copy is a student run graphic design business. We use several of the Adobe Creative Suite programs, such as, Photoshop, Illustrator, and inDesign. We practice our graphic design skills both in the classroom and the real world by reaching out to the community for projects.

We have designed business cards, t- shirts, posters, booklets, and more. To see the projects we have created, vist our projects page. For any business inquiries, visit our contact page.

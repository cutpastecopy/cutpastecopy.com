About Cut, Paste & Copy

Cut, Paste & Copy is a student run graphic design business. We use several of the Adobe Creative Suite programs, such as, Photoshop, Illustrator, and inDesign. To practice our graphic design skills, we reach out to the community for real world experience.

For our past community projects we have created business cards, t- shirt designs, posters, booklets, and more. If you would like to see the projects we have created, vist our projects page. Please feel free to go to our contact page to email us about any projects you would like us to create.
